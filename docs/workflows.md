# Workflows

## New Booking to Active Contract
1. Customer searches availability.
2. Customer selects vehicle.
3. Customer enters information and verifies OTP.
4. OCR extracts ID/license data if uploaded.
5. Payment link or direct payment is created.
6. Booking becomes confirmed.
7. Branch assigns a vehicle.
8. Staff completes handover checklist.
9. Customer signs.
10. Contract becomes active.

## Return Workflow
1. Staff opens return task.
2. Photos are captured.
3. AI vision compares before/after images.
4. Fuel, mileage and damages are calculated.
5. Extra charges are invoiced.
6. Contract is closed.
7. Vehicle goes to available, cleaning or maintenance.
